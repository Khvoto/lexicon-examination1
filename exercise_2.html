My favourite movies

  1. Lord of the Rings

    A fantastic triology, based on the works of JRR Tolkien with the same name. Follows the fellowships journey to destroy the one ring and save the world from Sauron once and for all.

  2. Star Wars

    Another great triology, a science fiction saga following a rag tag band of unlikely companions trying to create balance in the universe by toppling a dictator.

  3. Harry Potter

    Based on the books of JK Rowling, this series consists of eight movies, following Harry Potter during his school years, exploring love, rivalry, and finishes up with battling one of the greatest evil wizards of all time.
